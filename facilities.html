Detailed Facility Information

Internet & Connectivity

  • Speed: Up to 100 Mbps download / 20 Mbps upload
  • Coverage: All rooms and common areas
  • Devices: Connect up to 3 devices simultaneously
  • Support: IT support available during business hours
  • Fair Use: Unlimited for academic purposes

Security Features

  • Access Control: Biometric fingerprint system
  • Surveillance: 24/7 CCTV monitoring
  • Emergency: Panic buttons in strategic locations
  • Visitors: Strict visitor management system
  • Safety: Regular safety audits and drills

Academic Support Facilities

  • Study Rooms: 5 dedicated study rooms
  • Computer Lab: 24-hour computer access
  • Printing: Affordable printing services
  • Quiet Zones: Designated quiet study areas
  • Tutoring: Peer tutoring sessions available

Common Areas & Social Spaces

  • TV Lounge: Large screen TV with DSTV
  • Games Room: Pool table and board games
  • Outdoor Area: Braai facilities and seating
  • Kitchen: Fully equipped on each floor
  • Dining: Communal dining tables

Facility Usage Guidelines

  • Clean up after using any facility
  • Report any damages immediately
  • Respect quiet hours in study areas
  • No cooking appliances in bedrooms
  • Visitor access limited to common areas
  • Follow laundry room schedule
  • Security protocols must be followed
  • Wi-Fi for academic use prioritized
