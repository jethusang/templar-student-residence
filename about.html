Our Vision & Mission

Welcome to Templar Residence
Kimberley's Premier Student Home

Templar Residence is Crest Haven's largest student accommodation facility, strategically located in the heart of Kimberley's Central Business District. Designed to accommodate up to 375 students, we provide a safe, comfortable, and supportive living environment for Sol Plaatje University students.

Our residence offers more than just a place to sleep - we create a community where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally. With 24/7 security, modern facilities, and dedicated support staff, we ensure our residents have everything they need for a successful university experience.

Our Vision

To be a leading student accommodation provider that creates a supportive, purpose-driven environment, positively influencing students' lives and fostering a community where individuals can thrive and make a difference in the world.

Our Mission

To provide high quality, inclusive housing that not only meets students' needs, but also encourages personal growth, community building, and purposeful living. We are committed to creating a space where students are valued, empowered, and inspired to positively impact their futures and the world around them.
